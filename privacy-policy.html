1. Introduction

Welcome to Spendry ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have complete control over your financial data. This Privacy Policy explains how Spendry collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use our mobile application.

By using Spendry, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Our core philosophy is simple: You are the only owner of your data.

2. Information We Collect

Because Spendry is a local-storage application, the data collection is minimal and primarily functional.

2.1 Financial Data

You input various financial data into the app, including:

  • Transaction history (income and expenses)
  • Account balances and debt records
  • Budget limits and savings goals
  • Investment portfolio details

Storage: This data is stored locally on your device within the secure Application Sandbox provided by iOS. If you enable iCloud sync, a copy is stored in your private iCloud container, encrypted by Apple.

2.2 Biometric Data

If you enable "FaceID / TouchID Lock" in Settings:

  • The app uses the iOS LocalAuthentication framework.
  • We strictly receive only a "Success" or "Fail" signal from the operating system.
  • We never access, store, or transmit your actual biometric data (face or fingerprint data).

2.3 Usage Data

We do not use invasive third-party analytics platforms (like Google Analytics or Mixpanel) to track your individual behavior inside the app. We may collect anonymous, aggregated crash reports via Apple's built-in TestFlight or App Store metrics to improve app stability.

3. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of trusted third-party services for essential app functions:

3.1 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage In-App Purchases (Spendry Premium subscriptions). When you make a purchase:

  • RevenueCat receives an anonymous App User ID.
  • They process the receipt validation with the Apple App Store.
  • They do NOT receive your personal financial data, name, or exact location.

3.2 iCloud (Apple Inc.)

If CloudKit sync is enabled, your data is transferred to Apple's iCloud servers. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

We implement state-of-the-art security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption at Rest: The iOS file system encrypts your data on the device when it is locked.
  • Encryption in Transit: Any synchronization with iCloud occurs over SSL/TLS encrypted connections.
  • Sandbox Isolation: Other apps on your device cannot access Spendry's data container.

5. CCPA & GDPR Compliance

Although we do not process personal data on our own servers, we respect your rights under GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act).

Your Right to Access & Export

You can view all your stored data within the app at any time. We act as a tool for you to manage your own data.

Your Right to Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")

Since we do not have your data on our servers, you can delete your data completely by:

  • Deleting the Spendry app from your device.
  • Deleting the Spendry data from your iCloud Storage (Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage).

6. Children's Privacy

Spendry does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us ensuring no data was transmitted to us, but resides solely on their device.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: support@spendryapp.com
