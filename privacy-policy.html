Spendry is built on a "Local-First" architecture. We do not maintain servers to store your financial data. We cannot see, access, or sell your personal information because it never leaves your control.

1. Introduction

Spendry ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, and stored when you use the Spendry mobile application ("App").

2. Data Collection & Storage

Our guiding principle is data minimization. We only collect the absolute minimum data required for the App to function.

2.1 Financial Data

All financial transactions, budgets, debts, and account balances you enter into the App are stored locally on your device using Apple's secure Core Data / SwiftData framework. We do not have access to this data.

2.2 Usage Data

The App does not collect personally identifiable usage data. We do not use third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics or Firebase) that track your individual behavior.

3. iCloud Synchronization

If you enable iCloud synchronization, your data is transferred between your devices using Apple's CloudKit service. This process is governed by:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted by Apple both in transit and at rest on Apple's servers.
  • Access: The data is stored in your private iCloud container. Only you, via your Apple ID, can access it. We (the Developer) do not have access to your iCloud data.

4. Data Retention

Since we do not store your personal data on our servers, we do not have a data retention policy for your financial records. Your data remains on your device (and iCloud) until you choose to delete it.

5. Use of Data

We use the data solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality (e.g., calculating your budget).
  • Synchronize your data across your devices (via iCloud).
  • Provide customer support (only if you voluntarily email us screenshots or logs).

6. Disclosure of Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

Law Enforcement: We may be required to disclose data if required by law. However, because we strictly do not possess your financial data or encryption keys due to our Zero-Knowledge architecture, we generally cannot provide user data in response to legal requests.

7. Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We leverage iOS's native security features, including:

  • Sandboxing: App data is isolated from other apps on your device.
  • Biometrics: You can secure app access using Face ID or Touch ID. This processing happens entirely on-device via the Secure Enclave.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us.

9. Your Data Rights

Under laws like GDPR and CCPA, you have specific rights regarding your data. Since your data is stored locally, you exercise these rights directly on your device:

  • Right to Access: You can see all your data simply by opening the App.
  • Right to Rectification: You can edit any transaction or budget directly within the App.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can delete all your data by deleting the App from your device and deleting the "Spendry" data from your iCloud Storage settings.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
